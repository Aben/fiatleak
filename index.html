fiatleak - watch the world's currencies flow into BTC in realtime

When one of the fiat currencies listed below is used to purchase BTC on the mt.gox market, a bitcoin is sent from the originating country to the currency counter in red. The total BTC value is listed in green, this value is also tracked for each currency.

Refreshing the site will cause all data to be reset to 0, allowing you to track differences in time between currencies.
